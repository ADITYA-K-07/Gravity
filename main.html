Our Story

Built by a Fitness Enthusiast.
For Every Body.

Gravity Fitness Club was founded in 2010 by Mr. Sameet Gawand — a fitness enthusiast with a simple but powerful vision: create a space where anyone, regardless of age or ability, can pursue their best physical self.

Over 15 years, we've grown from a neighbourhood gym into Bibwewadi's most trusted fitness institution — with over 1,200 members who swear by our trainers, equipment, and welcoming atmosphere.

We believe fitness isn't a luxury. It's a lifestyle — and we make it accessible, sustainable, and genuinely enjoyable.
